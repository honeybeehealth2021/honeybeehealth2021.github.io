Honey bee Health in A Changing World Mini-Symposia 2021

Ecology and Evolution Unit, Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology
22-26 March 2021

& e-COLOSS Asia, Okinawa

As 2020 showed us clearly, our world is ever changing. Admid the COVID world crisis, we will be delighted to host an online mini-symposia on the theme of "Honey Bee Health in A Changing World" and gather bee researchers as best as possible. The 2nd edition of COLOSS Asia will be jointly hosted by our Ecology and Evolution Unit in Okinawa.
